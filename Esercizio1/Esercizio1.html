Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries,and Brie

Wow,your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries,and creamy brie,if will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image:Sally Vegas

Fresh Cramberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients:

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil,plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions,thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie,cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring kinfe if desidered,thought it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    in a medium-sized mixing bowl,or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix a togheter all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.you don't need to actually knead the dough, tough i sometimes kneaad it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
